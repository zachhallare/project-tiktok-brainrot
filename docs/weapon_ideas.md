Sword — The baseline. Balanced everything. No changes. The benchmark other weapons are measured against.

Spear — Range specialist. The shaft is just wood — only the last 15% of the blade counts as a sweet spot. But that tip hits for double damage. Rewarding if you control spacing, terrible up close.

Reach: very long (~85px)
Spin: slightly slower (shaft is heavy)
Grinding damage: near zero
Tip damage: doubled
Sweet spot threshold pushed to 85%+ of blade length


Dagger — Fast and relentless. Too short to zone but spins so fast it overwhelms parry energy quickly. The enemy burns through their energy bar and gets guard broken.

Reach: short (~30px)
Spin speed: very fast
Damage: low per hit
Parry cost to the defender: cheaper to parry but hits so frequently it drains energy fast


Axe — Slow but every hit hurts. No grinding hits — the whole blade counts as sweet spot. One clean connection does serious damage and knockback.

Reach: medium
Spin: slow
All hits treated as sweet spot regardless of impact ratio
Knockback: 2x


Hammer — Doesn't cut, just launches. Low damage but on any hit it reverses the opponent's spin direction (disorientation) and applies maximum hitstop. Turns the fight into chaos.

Reach: short-medium
Spin: very slow
Damage: very low
On hit: opponent spin_direction *= -1
Hitstop: maximum every time
